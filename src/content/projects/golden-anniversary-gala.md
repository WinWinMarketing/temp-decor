Fifty years of marriage deserved nothing less than a celebration as extraordinary as the love it honored. The Martinelli family wanted a golden gala that felt like the finest evening their guests had ever experienced.

We transformed the Liberty Grand's Renaissance Hall into a symphony of gold. From the 8-foot gold floral arrangements flanking the entrance to the custom gold-sequin table linens, every surface caught and reflected the warm candlelight. Over 500 taper candles created a sea of flickering light across the room.

A live jazz quartet played beneath a custom-built stage backdrop of gold geometric frames interspersed with cascading white orchids. The dance floor was encircled by towering floral columns, each featuring a curated mix of gold spray roses, white hydrangeas, and preserved eucalyptus.

The pièce de résistance was a retrospective gallery corridor — a 40-foot passage lined with framed photographs from each decade of the Martinellis' marriage, illuminated by museum-quality spotlights and flanked by gold urns of white calla lilies. Guests wandered through the timeline of love before entering the main celebration.
